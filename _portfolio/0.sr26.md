<h2>Design Work</h2>
<p> In my third year on the team, I worked on designing the rear transfer case, which uses bevel gears to transmit torque from the gearbox to the propshaft, which ultimately drives the 4-wheel drive system. I used Siemens NX to design the parts. Learning to analyze bevel gears was difficult, but rewarding, and I used both textbooks (Shigley's), as well as software including Kissoft and Ansys, to analyze the gears and housing. </p>
