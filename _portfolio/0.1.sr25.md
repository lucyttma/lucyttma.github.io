<h2> Design Work</h2>
<p> In my second year on the team, I focused primarily on designing drivetrain components. I designed the universal joints that interface with the gearbox to transmit torque to the load-bearing rear half-shafts, as well as the front spindle yoke that interfaces with the wheel. In doing so, I utilized Siemens NX to design the CAD models of the parts, as well as Ansys to analyize the different parts. </p>
